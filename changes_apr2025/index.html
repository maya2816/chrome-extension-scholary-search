About ScholarSearch

ScholarSearch is a Chrome extension designed to streamline your academic research process. By leveraging AI and Semantic Scholar integration, it helps you quickly identify and evaluate the most relevant articles from Google Scholar.

How to Use

  1. Start browsing Google Scholar
  2. Click the ScholarSearch icon in the toolbar.
  3. Enter your search keywords and a research query.
  4. Review the top 10 re-ranked results and their summaries.
  5. Click on the links to articles. Happy researching!
